About SkillMatch Agency

SkillMatch Agency is a Rwanda-based internship placement agency that connects businesses with motivated individuals while giving young professionals meaningful opportunities to grow, develop their skills, and apply them through real-world experience.

Our Approach

We prepare and support interns through mentorship, workshops, and a network hub of professionals.

Results That Matter

Businesses get structured support, interns contribute from day one, and professionals gain clear growth pathways.

Our Reach Across Africa

Opportunities in Rwanda, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, Nigeria, Côte d’Ivoire, Kenya, and Gabon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some common queries.

What is SkillMatch? +

SkillMatch is a premier internship placement agency based in Rwanda. We connect talented individuals, from students to young professionals, with meaningful internship opportunities at top companies to help them gain real-world experience and launch their careers.

How does the application process work? +

You can start by exploring our open internship positions or joining one of our Focus area. Once you apply, our team reviews your profile to match you with the best-fit opportunities. We then guide you through the interview and placement process with the host company.

Are the internships offered through SkillMatch paid? +

Many of our internship opportunities offer a stipend or are paid positions. The specifics of compensation depend on the host company and the nature of the internship. We are committed to finding valuable and fair opportunities for our candidates.

Who is eligible to apply for internships? +

We welcome applications from university students, recent graduates, and young professionals looking to gain practical experience in their chosen field. Specific eligibility criteria may vary depending on the requirements of the host company for each role.
